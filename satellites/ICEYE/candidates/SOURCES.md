# ICEYE — Candidate Reference Images

**Mission:** ICEYE SAR constellation  
**Company:** ICEYE (Finnish commercial operator)  
**Band:** X-band SAR  
**First launch:** January 2018; constellation now exceeds 30 satellites  
**Note:** ICEYE is one of the leading commercial SAR providers. The satellites are small (≈100 kg microsatellites) with a distinctive compact body and deployable SAR antenna. Later Gen-4 spacecraft are larger with improved performance.

## Priority Image Sources

ICEYE has an official media assets page with downloadable press imagery — the best source for high-quality spacecraft renders:

**ICEYE Official Media Assets (best starting point):**  
→ https://www.iceye.com/newsroom/media-assets  
Look for: "ICEYE SAR satellite" images and spacecraft renders — these are explicitly offered for press use.

**eoPortal (detailed technical imagery):**  
→ https://www.eoportal.org/satellite-missions/iceye-constellation  
Contains spacecraft schematics, antenna diagrams, and mission overview imagery.

**ESA Earth Online:**

**Wikipedia:**

## Recommended Images to Download

1. From ICEYE media assets page: the "SAR satellite" render (typically a side view of the deployed satellite in orbit)
2. From eoPortal: any spacecraft diagram showing the deployed antenna configuration

## Notes for SVG Artist

ICEYE satellites are box-shaped microsatellites with a large deployable slotted waveguide SAR antenna extending to one side. Gen-1/2/3 are compact (~70 cm × 80 cm bus); Gen-4 is larger. Required files:
- `ICEYE.svg`
- `ICEYE-icon.svg`
- `ICEYE-512px.png`
- `ICEYE-1024px.png`
