# Capella-2 (Sequoia) — Candidate Reference Images

**Mission:** Capella-2, operational name "Sequoia"  
**Company:** Capella Space (US commercial operator)  
**Band:** X-band SAR  
**Launched:** 31 August 2020 (the first fully operational Capella SAR satellite)  
**Note:** Capella-2 "Sequoia" was the first fully commercial US SAR satellite. Capella's constellation now includes Capella-3 through Capella-12. The design features a hexagonal bus with a deployable SAR reflector dish — visually distinctive from the flat-panel ICEYE/NovaSAR approach.

## Notes for SVG Artist

Capella-2 has a distinctive compact hexagonal bus with a large deployable parabolic/reflector SAR antenna — quite different in shape from flat-panel SAR satellites. The reflector dish folds up for launch and deploys in orbit. Required files:
- `Capella-2.svg`
- `Capella-2-icon.svg`
- `Capella-2-512px.png`
