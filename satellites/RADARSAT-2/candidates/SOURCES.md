# RADARSAT-2 — Candidate Reference Images

**Mission:** RADARSAT-2  
**Agency:** MDA Space / Canadian Space Agency (CSA)  
**Band:** C-band SAR  
**Launched:** 14 December 2007 (still operational)  
**Note:** RADARSAT-2 is one of the world's most widely used commercial SAR satellites. Built by MDA Space, it features a large C-band SAR antenna and three large solar panels in a distinctive configuration. One of the most commercially successful SAR missions.

## Notes for SVG Artist

RADARSAT-2 has a large rectangular C-band SAR antenna panel (~15 m × 1.5 m) as its most prominent feature, plus three large solar panel arrays. The overall form factor is large and rectangular — distinct from the more compact X-band SAR platforms. Required files:
