# SAOCOM-1B — Candidate Reference Images

**Mission:** SAOCOM-1B (Satélite Argentino de Observación COn Microondas)  
**Agency:** CONAE (Argentina) / INVAP  
**Band:** L-band SAR  
**Launched:** 30 August 2020  
**Note:** SAOCOM-1B is the companion satellite to SAOCOM-1A (already in this repo). They share the same bus and antenna design — the SAOCOM-1A SVG is a strong starting point with only minor differences.
